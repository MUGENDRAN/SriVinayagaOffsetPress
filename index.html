ABOUT US

Welcome to our Offset Press Shop!

We are a dedicated functional unit committed to providing top-notch services related to press jobs. Our team of experienced professionals uses state-of-the-art offset printing technology to deliver high-quality print materials.

1. Pre-press Services : Our pre-press services ensure that your designs are print-ready. We offer design assistance, digital proofing, and plate making.

2. Offset Printing: We specialize in offset printing which allows for high-quality, sharp, and clean images and typefaces. Whether it's brochures, business cards, posters, or books, we've got you covered.

3. Post-press Services: Our job doesn't end at printing. We provide finishing services such as cutting, folding, binding, and packaging to give your printed materials a professional look.
